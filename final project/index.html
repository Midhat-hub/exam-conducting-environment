About TestSphere

TestSphere is a cutting-edge platform designed to provide a secure and transparent environment for conducting various tests. By utilizing blockchain technology, we ensure that all test data is immutable and verifiable, offering unparalleled trust and reliability.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does TestSphere prevent cheating?

We utilize the Browser Visibility API to monitor user activity and detect any suspicious behavior during exams, such as tab switching or screen locking.

Is TestSphere secure?

Yes, TestSphere leverages blockchain technology to ensure data integrity and security, making all test records immutable and verifiable.

Can I customize my exams?

Absolutely! TestSphere allows you to design and customize your exams to fit your specific requirements, including question types and grading criteria.
